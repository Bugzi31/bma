Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of marketing services, including digital marketing, social media management, search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, content creation, pr management, email marketing, and branding solutions. We also provide tailored marketing strategies to help businesses grow.

BMA Marketing Limited is based in Leicester/United Kingdom. We serve clients across the UK.

We work with businesses of all sizes. Whether you're a startup, a small business, or a well-established company, we can create a marketing strategy tailored to your needs.

Digital marketing is the use of online platforms, tools, and strategies to promote your business. It helps you reach a larger audience, drive traffic to your website, and increase sales by targeting potential customers where they spend their time online.

We use key performance indicators (KPIs) such as website traffic, click-through rates (CTR), conversion rates, ROI, and engagement metrics to measure the success of our campaigns. Detailed reports are provided regularly to ensure transparency.

Yes! We can manage your social media accounts, create engaging posts, and run targeted advertising campaigns to increase your brand’s visibility and engagement with your audience.

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) involves optimizing your website to rank higher on search engine results pages. This increases organic traffic and helps potential customers find your business more easily.

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results may take 3–6 months to become noticeable. However, the impact is lasting and significantly improves your online visibility over time.

PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising is a paid marketing strategy where you only pay when someone clicks on your ad. It’s an effective way to drive targeted traffic to your website quickly.

PR (Public Relations) management involves building and maintaining a positive image for your business through media relations, press releases, event management, and crisis communication. At BMA Marketing Limited, we craft compelling PR strategies to enhance your brand reputation, manage public perception, and ensure effective communication with your audience and the media.
